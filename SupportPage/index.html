Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a game?

First, add all players and assign roles. Then tap "Начать игру" (Start Game). Make sure at least one Mafia role is included.

Why is the "Start Game" button not working?

The button only activates when roles are properly assigned. Make sure you've added enough players and that at least one Mafia or Don role is present.

Can I record or play audio?

The app uses sound effects, but does not record audio. Microphone access is not required.

Where are the photos stored?

Photos taken in the app are stored securely using your device's photo library permissions and Firebase Storage.
